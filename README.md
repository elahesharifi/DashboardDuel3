## ğŸ” Key Insights
Developed in collaboration with **Helene Hinskens**, this dashboard utilizes **real-world data** to provide deep insights into student success.

### 1. Career Transition (Sankey Diagram)
A standout feature of this analysis is the visualization of how talent pivots from fields such as **Administration, Marketing, Logistics, and Research** into high-demand IT specializations including:
* **Web Development & Full Stack Development** (Largest talent pool).
* **Data Analytics & Data Science**.
* **Cybersecurity & UI/UX**.

### 2. Language Proficiency & Employment
* **English**: 75% of talents possess a C1/C2 level, making them highly suitable for international tech environments.
* **Dutch**: 50% of students are currently at A1 or A2 levels. This highlights a strategic opportunity for language development to boost success in the local Dutch market.

### 3. Top Tech Academies
The dashboard tracks the primary educational sources for TechMeUp talent, with **Ironhack** and **Le Wagon** leading as the top contributors of graduates.
